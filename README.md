# AI Project: Smart Study Assistant

## Summary
This project is an AI-powered study assistant that helps students organize their study time, create personalized study plans, and answer simple questions about their subjects.

## Background
Many students struggle with time management and finding effective ways to study. This project aims to reduce stress and improve learning efficiency.

## How is it used?
Students enter their subjects, available study time, and upcoming exams. The AI suggests a study schedule and learning priorities.

## Data sources and AI methods
The system uses:
- Student study schedules
- Exam dates
- Learning progress

Possible AI methods:
- Recommendation systems
- Machine learning
- Natural language processing

## Challenges
The AI may not always understand each student's learning style perfectly, and recommendations depend on the quality of the provided data.

## What next?
Future versions could include voice support, reminders, and integration with school platforms.
